Overview of Our Business

Text and tool hub (U) Limited is dedicated to providing quality educational materials, stationery, and lab equipment to students, schools, and educators. Located just 6 km from Kampala City Centre on Bombo Road, we strive to make learning affordable and accessible to all.

Background

Since our inception, Text and tool hub has been committed to supporting the academic growth of students in Uganda and beyond. With a wide range of products tailored for learners of all levels, we aim to empower the next generation of scientists, engineers, and scholars.
